About

For 24 hours, ImplementAI will have 100 participants collaborate and build applications around the latest technology AI has to offer. The hackathon will bring together researchers from the best AI labs in the world, companies and students to explore the implementations of the latest research in AI.
During your time here, you will meet other people passionate about AI and learn from the best mentors the industry has to offer. We will offer free food, super cool merchandise (aka: SWAG!) awesome prizes and more food.
Whether your are an AI novice or a veteran you will be given the tools, data-sets and guidance to develop your skills and leave knowing you are a better programmer than you were yesterday.

FAQ

I am completely new to AI and I am nervous.

Have no fear! ImplementAI is tailored to all skill levels. And more importantly, we are first and foremost a learning experience that is meant to be fun!

Where is the event taking place?

We wanted to ensure that you guys would have a comfortable place to hack. So we booked Notman House for the event! Go ahead and take the 3D tour and check it out!

What should I bring?

Bring your student ID and you laptop. Don't forget your chargers! Toiletries are also recommended for hygiene.

Will this cost money?

Nope! This event is 100% free. We will supply swag and food at no cost. However, we will not provide housing or transportation reimbursement.

Who can attend?

Both undergraduate and graduate at any University in the Montreal Area.

What is the schedule?

On Friday night, we will be holding several tech talks followed by a networking event. Then Saturday Morning is when the hacking begins. Finally, we will announce the winners and distribute prizes Sunday Afternoon.
